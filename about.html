Our Story

How Elevate Reads Was Born

Elevate Reads did not start as a business plan. It started as a frustration.

Several years ago, our founder was exactly where you might be now: an indie author with a great book and absolutely no idea how to market it. The book was good. Really good. Beta readers loved it. Professional editors polished it. A talented designer created a stunning cover.

And then... nothing. The book launched to crickets.

Friends and family bought a few copies. A handful of random Amazon shoppers stumbled across it. But the thousands of readers who would have loved the book? They never found it.

That is when the realization hit: thousands of talented indie authors are in this exact same boat. They have written amazing books that readers would love, but those books are invisible because their authors do not have marketing expertise.

Elevate Reads was founded to solve this exact problem. To give indie authors access to professional, effective, specialized book marketing without requiring them to become marketing experts themselves.

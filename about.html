Our Story

How Elevate Reads Was Born

My name is Kolade Sebastian, and I founded Elevate Reads because I kept seeing the same painful pattern repeat itself. Authors with extraordinary knowledge, decades of hard-earned expertise, and books that genuinely deserved to be read, were watching those books disappear into obscurity. Not because the content was weak. But because the gap between what their books contained and how those books were presenting themselves online had never been properly closed. I built Elevate Reads specifically to close that gap.

The book was good. Really good. Beta readers loved it. Professional editors polished it. A talented designer created a stunning cover. Everything was ready. And then nothing. The book launched to crickets. Friends and family bought a few copies. A handful of readers stumbled across it. But the thousands of readers who would have loved the book? They never found it.

That is where we come in. Elevate Reads is a positioning practice built specifically for non-fiction authors, business thought leaders, and leadership experts. We have studied the professional publishing landscape, mastered Amazon's algorithm as it applies to expert and authority-based books, and developed strategies that actually work for authors whose books carry institutional credibility that their online presence has never properly reflected.

We do not offer one-size-fits-all solutions. We offer partnerships. When you work with Elevate Reads, you are not buying a service off a shelf. You are gaining a strategic partner who genuinely cares about your success and who constructs everything from scratch around your specific book, your specific credentials, and your specific reader.

Our Mission

What Drives Every Decision We Make

"Our mission is to give every non-fiction author, business thought leader, and leadership expert a fair shot at reaching the readers, organizations, and institutions their books were written for. Through honest strategy, precise execution, and genuine long-term partnership."

Expertise Should Not Be Limited by Invisibility

The best business books do not automatically reach the executives, academics, and organizations that need them most. Strategic positioning makes the difference. We exist to ensure that authors with genuine authority have the visibility infrastructure their expertise deserves.

Authors Should Focus on Their Work

You became a non-fiction author because you have something important to say to your field. Not because you wanted to master Amazon algorithms or build content calendars. We handle the positioning strategy so you can focus on what you do best.

Transparency Is Non-Negotiable

You will always know exactly what we are doing, why we are doing it, and what results it is producing. No black boxes. No vague reports. No overpromising. Just honest, transparent partnership from start to finish.

Results Over Vanity Metrics

We do not celebrate impressions, reach, or follower counts unless those numbers are translating into actual book discovery and sales. We track the metrics that matter. Ranking. Discoverability. Revenue. Inquiries from the right professional channels.

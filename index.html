The Challenge

The Truth About Being a
Non-Fiction Author Today

You have spent years, perhaps decades, building your expertise. You have worked with organizations, led teams, advised executives, published research, and earned a level of authority that very few people in your field can match. Then you wrote a book. And almost nothing happened.

Not because the book was weak. Your content is exceptional. But because publishing a book and positioning a book are two completely different disciplines.

📄

Your Amazon Page Is Not Doing Its Job

Most non-fiction authors upload their book to Amazon with a description that reads like an academic abstract or a back-cover summary. Neither format surfaces books through Amazon's algorithm, and neither is how a busy executive or corporate buyer decides to purchase.

🎓

Your Credentials Are Buried or Missing

You have decades of expertise, institutional endorsements, and professional credibility that your ideal reader would find immediately compelling. But if those assets are not positioned correctly, they are invisible. They exist in your bio. They do not exist in your discovery strategy.

🎯

You Are Not Reaching the Right Channels

The readers who would benefit most from your book are not browsing Amazon the way fiction readers do. They are making bulk purchase decisions for their teams. They are looking for books for corporate programs, academic syllabi, and leadership development curricula.

📊

Your Book Is Competing Against the Wrong Titles

If your book is placed in the wrong Amazon categories, you are competing against titles that attract a completely different reader. Correct category placement and keyword strategy determine whether your book surfaces to the right professional audience.

The Solution

What Happens When Your Book Is Properly Positioned

Imagine a chief learning officer at a Fortune 500 company searching Amazon for exactly the topic your book addresses, finding your title on the first page of results, and ordering fifty copies for a leadership program.

Imagine a university professor finding your book correctly categorized in a way that makes its academic application immediately clear. That is not fantasy. That is what happens when your book has been properly positioned.

  • ✓Amazon page restructured to lead with authority and convert professional browsers into buyers
  • ✓Correct category and keyword placement for professional and institutional discoverability
  • ✓Corporate and academic channel outreach strategy
  • ✓Transparent reporting on results throughout every stage of our engagement

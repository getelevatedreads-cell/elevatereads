FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

What happens after I submit the contact form?

We will respond within 24 to 48 hours with answers to your questions, relevant information about services you asked about, and if appropriate, an invitation to book a free strategy call. No pressure, just helpful information.

I am not ready to hire anyone. Should I still reach out?

Absolutely! We are happy to answer general questions, provide strategic advice, and help you understand what you need. We do not pressure anyone. If you are just gathering information, that is perfectly fine!

Do you work with authors outside the US?

Yes! We work with authors across six continents. Communication via email and video calls works across time zones. Amazon and social media are global, and most of our strategies work worldwide.

How long before I see results?

It depends on the service and your starting point. Amazon optimization often shows ranking improvements within 2 to 4 weeks. Book marketing typically shows sales impact within 30 days. Social media growth builds over 3 to 6 months. We set realistic expectations during your strategy call.

Do you work with all genres?

Yes! We have successfully worked with fiction (romance, thriller, mystery, fantasy, sci-fi, contemporary, and more) and non-fiction (business, self-help, memoir, how-to, health, and more). Each genre has unique strategies, and we customize our approach accordingly.

Will you share my information?

Never. We do not sell or share contact information, we do not spam, and we respect your privacy completely. Your information is safe with us.
